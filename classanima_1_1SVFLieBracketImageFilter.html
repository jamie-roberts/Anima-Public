Detailed Description

template<typename TPixelType, unsigned int Dimension>
class anima::SVFLieBracketImageFilter< TPixelType, Dimension >

Computes the Lie bracket between two fields u and v as expressed by Bossa et al.

There is a discrepancy in between Vercauteren et al. and Bossa et al. formulation. This Lie bracket implements the Vercauteren et al. formulation [u,v](x) = Jac(u)(x).v(x) - Jac(v)(x).u(x) M. Bossa et al. "Contributions to 3D diffeomorphic atlas estimation : application to brain images.", MICCAI 2007, p. 667â€“674. T. Vercauteren et al. "Symmetric Log-Domain Diffeomorphic Registration: A Demons-based Approach.", MICCAI 2008, p. 754-761.
